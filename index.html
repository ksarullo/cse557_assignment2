**Please read before using**

Users can select:

  • One or more location(s) to view location data, or
  • One or more employee(s) to view employee data, or
  • One or more location(s) and employee(s) to view employee data at a location, or
  • One employee to see GPS route history, and
  • Filter all data by date

Switch to the Filter Tab when you are ready to make selections.

Switch to the Analysis Tab when you are done making your selections.
